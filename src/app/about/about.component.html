We believe in a future of real estate where everyone has a great experience in finding what they’re looking for. It should be as simple as a click of a button to put time, energy, and resources back into peoples’ hands, making it so that the search is the easiest part of the property purchase or rental process.

Our mission

Our mission is to make the agent-to-client dynamic more authentic, human, and real by encouraging the agent to reach out to the client. In doing so, we shift the focus from the listing, to the connection that takes place between people. We believe that the real estate dynamic should be about people working together to find what they need.

Our vision

Our vision is to simplify your real estate search. Whether our users are searching to buy a home or rent an apartment, or if they’re real estate agents searching for serious clients, we are building a future where everyones’ needs are fulfilled with ease, simplicity, and satisfaction.

Our purpose:

Innovate the NYC real estate search to return time, energy, and effort to real estate agents and clients. The idea for SpotLeads originated when founder Sivan Aharon was experiencing the painful and drawn-out process of finding an NYC apartment. After spending a frustrating number of hours searching for a place, SpotLeads was born with the conclusion that clients simply shouldn’t be playing the role of a real estate agent. We believe that our client users have enough to worry about for their upcoming move—and that they can’t afford to endlessly scroll for hours on end. Our ”why” is to offer a platform that takes this burden off the client, allowing the professionals to do what they are here to do.

Bringing the “real” back to real estate.

SpotLeads believes that real estate should be about people coming together to find what they need. Our app is innovating how agents and clients find each other, as we believe the search for your next dream home or client should be simple, efficient, and satisfying.
